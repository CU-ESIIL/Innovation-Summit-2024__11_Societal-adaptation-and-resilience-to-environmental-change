2018 Arizona Population Density

This dashboard shows Arizona's 2018 population density in people per sq. km. Visual trends show that Arizona's population is largely concentrated in large metropolitan areas, whereas rural block groups tend to have very low population density, as low as 2 people per square kilometer.

 

Usage notes:

  • Use the county filter above to zoom and pan to a specific county. 
  • Click on a county to see a population breakdown pop-up. 
  • Use the layers button at the top right to see detailed race density maps.

Data source: 2018 US Census Bureau TIGER/Line data, 2018 American Community Survey
